About CrateSpace

Is a Warehouse


The CrateSpace warehouse is:

  • Constructed with insulated panels for eco-friendly climate control.
  • Surveilled by 24 hour, 360-degree security cameras.
  • Equipped with a truck-friendly loading dock.
  • Clean, organized, and well-designed.

The warehouse was designed to maximize receiving and storing efficiencies to provide the best value to our customers. The fully enclosed, high efficiency heating and cooling system keeps our customer's products room temperature during the hot summers and cold winters.

Is a Business


The CrateSpace business:

  • Communicates with customers in real-time so they know what has arrived.
  • Ensures that product received is not damaged and matches the Bill of Lading.
  • Notates on the Bill of Lading any box damage.
  • Opens and thoroughly inspects all boxes containing product with marble, stone, or glass.
  • Provides bathrooms, water, and snacks for drivers to maximize friendly relationships.

The CrateSpace business was created to remove the hassle of receiving and storing your product so that you can focus on your core business without distraction. Customer satisfaction is our priority. If receiving and storing product has been a barrier to growing your business, we promise to turn it into a great experience for you and save you money.

Is a Concept


The CrateSpace concept is:

  • A "CrateSpace" or a 4' x 4' pallet sized footprint, up to 7.5' tall (16 ft2).
  • Not confined, so larger product can fit into 2 or 3 "CrateSpaces" for example.
  • Able to be rented by the day or by the month.

The CrateSpace concept was made to scale space and time, tailored to customer needs. The goal is to provide money saving efficiencies while minimizing wasted efforts by the customer. The CrateSpace concept allows for short-term or long-term storage and allows customers to only pay for what space they use as they receive product for a project.
